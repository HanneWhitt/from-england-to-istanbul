{% block title %}My name is Hannes, and I'm taking my bike on a solo journey of 5,000km from my hometown of Cambridge, UK, to Istanbul. {% endblock %}


Beginning on 23rd August 2023, it will be a long, arduous, exhausting, painful, and wonderful journey of one man, one bike, and one overcomplicated camping hammock. I am doing it to try to raise $12,000 to support three charities: Helen Keller International's vitamin A supplementation program, the Founders Pledge Climate Change Fund, and StrongMinds.




Each cause is quite different, but they have one thing in common: they are chosen so as to do as much good as possible with the money, on the basis of strong evidence collected by Giving What We Can and other independent evaluators. No wishful thinking, corruption or administrative inefficiencies here; only real people and real help.

If you make a donation, be sure to let me know and post a message via this form! It means a huge amount to me personally to have your support on my long journey, and creates a buzz that encourages others to help too. If you wish, you can choose to remain anonymous and just post a message, or even just register your donation.

If you have any questions, or you'd prefer to let me know by email, just send me a message at hannes.whittingham@gmail.com!
